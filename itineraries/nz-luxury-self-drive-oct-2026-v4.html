The Journey

21 days across
North & South Islands

This itinerary moves you through the full sweep of Aotearoa: beginning in Auckland's harbour-side city, threading through the geothermal heart of Rotorua and Taupō, and crossing to the South Island for Abel Tasman, Marlborough wine country, the rugged coast at Kaikōura, and the alpine drama of Tekapo, Mount Cook and Wanaka before closing in adventure-capital Queenstown.

It is a self-drive trip designed for a group of eight, with two premium 4WDs, two carefully placed flight legs, and a mix of villas, apartments and hotels.

A note from Kristin

This itinerary moves through New Zealand at a comfortable pace across three weeks. Together with our local partner who specialises in self-drive tours, we've chosen properties that are comfortable and full of character, many of them set against vistas you'll only find here. Where possible, we've kept the eight of you under one roof. The logistics are taken care of, so you can immerse yourself in the sights and sounds of New Zealand.

We would gently recommend confirming soon, as availability of good accommodation for a group of eight is limited in October. We'd welcome any thoughts or feedback as you go over the itinerary.
