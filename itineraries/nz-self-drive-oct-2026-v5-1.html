The Journey

21 days across
North & South Islands

This itinerary moves you through the full sweep of Aotearoa: beginning in Auckland's downtown Britomart precinct, through the geothermal heart of Rotorua and the alpine slopes of Tongariro, then by air to Christchurch and the South Island for Mackenzie Country, Aoraki Mt Cook, Wānaka, Te Anau and Milford Sound, closing in adventure-capital Queenstown.

It is a self-drive trip designed for a group of eight, with two premium 4WDs, a short domestic flight to save the long Cook Strait day, and a mix of hotels, alpine lodges and lakefront apartments along the way, before another domestic flight to take you back to Auckland for your onward departure back to Singapore.

A note from Kristin

Together with our New Zealand specialist partner NZ Road Trip, we've put together a trip that moves at a comfortable pace across three weeks, with a route that keeps long-driving days short. Where available, we have selected 2 Bed 2 Bath apartments, and have added significantly more inclusions compared with the previous itinerary, while remaining within a SGD 8,900 – 9,500 per pax.

As you go through the itinerary, you'll see a Gold-tier upgrade option in Wānaka (Marina Terraces) and Queenstown (Matakauri House). Matakauri House in particular features stunning views of the Southern Alps, and as a 4 Bed 4 Bath villa would mark a nice end to the trip.
